    Morgan D is a clothing brand that was started in the year 2017 by a young entrepreneur who goes by the name David Morgan Lebese.

    Get Started

    Morgan D is a premium fashion brand that started out as a "thought" but has developed a loyal fan base following in the 2 years since its inception

    Get Started

    Morgan D was founded in 2017 by an undergraduate student, who at present is a Bcom student. Morgan D was born after David (the founder) and a friend tried their luck in the fashion industry but fell through

    Get Started

    With the amount of knowledge that David had obtained from the process, short falling, he had the urge to go deeper and pursue his vision, with his first Grapic T-shirts prints and headwear, which he went to sell to his friends

    Get Started

    Morgan D offers a great range of products from T-Shirts and Baseball Caps mode cotton adjustable caps to bucket hats with the use of embroidery.

    About Us

    Our Mission

    Morgan D does not only focus on providing a new look to consumers, as we are already living in the world of high velocity change, but bringing back the Gianni Versace with a great amount luxury provided to its valuable consumers

    Objective/Goal

    The brand's goal is in the next coming years to make an overwhelming impact on the fashion industry and create a large consumer demand for its products.Basically the goal in the next 2-5 years is to venture into women's and children's clothing.

    Strategic Relationship

    Morgan D has a strategic alliance with local DJs and the entertainment as a whole. These alliances are valuable to the brand because they provide the needed exposure for its line
